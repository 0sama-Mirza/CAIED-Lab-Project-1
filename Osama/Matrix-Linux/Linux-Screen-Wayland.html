Screen Recording On Gnome Wayland

We are going to use ffmpeg and another software to capture the screen and mic/computer's sound. ffmpeg will display us the video of the camera while the software will do all of the recording.

Use Kooha

Click Here To Download Kooha Screen Recorder Software Or Use The Followig Commands For Your Distro:

  • Arch:

    sudo pacman -S kooha

  • Flatpak/Others:

    flatpak install flathub io.github.seadve.Kooha

ffmpeg

Now To Capture The Camera Of Your Laptop Use The Followig Command:

  • ffplay /dev/video0

  • To make the stream better use this:

    ffplay -fflags nobuffer -flags low_delay -vf "setpts=N/60/TB" -noframedrop /dev/video0
